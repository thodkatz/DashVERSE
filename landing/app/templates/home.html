About DashVERSE

DashVERSE provides visual insights into research software quality assessments based on the EVERSE quality framework. The dashboards display metrics collected from software quality evaluation tools and map them to quality dimensions and indicators. The underlying data is generated by the QualityPipelines framework and structured using EVERSE schemas.
